# Glossary

**Domain Pack** — A modular bundle of rules, mappings, vocabularies, and constraints for a specific domain.

**Resolver** — A rule‑driven component that resolves ambiguity or applies domain logic.

**Edition** — A versioned rule‑set ensuring deterministic behaviour across releases.

**Pipeline** — The orchestrated sequence of transformation stages.

**Context** — The normalised representation of input before processing.

**Trace** — A structured record of all decisions made during transformation.
