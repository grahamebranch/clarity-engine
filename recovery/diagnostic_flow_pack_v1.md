# Diagnostic Flow Pack (v1) — Clarity Engine

## 1. Purpose
The Diagnostic Flow Pack provides structured, CEFR‑aligned diagnostic processes that determine:

- learner level  
- learner needs  
- learner gaps  
- learner goals  
- recommended lesson pathways  

This is a Phase‑4 extension and does not modify system‑layer behaviour.

---

## 2. Flow Identity

### 2.1 Name
**Diagnostic Flow Pack**

### 2.2 Scope
Used at:
- onboarding  
- programme entry  
- mid‑programme review  
- corporate training intake  

### 2.3 Output Types
- CEFR level estimate  
- skill‑gap profile  
- recommended domain  
- recommended edition  
- recommended lesson sequence  

---

## 3. Diagnostic Flow Structure (Authoritative)

All diagnostic flows must follow this structure:

1. **Intake Questions**  
2. **Language Sample**  
3. **Targeted Micro‑Tasks**  
4. **Error Pattern Analysis**  
5. **CEFR Alignment Check**  
6. **Needs Assessment**  
7. **Recommendation Engine Output**  

---

## 4. Flow Templates

### 4.1 General English Diagnostic
- warm‑up questions  
- short speaking sample

- listening or reading snippet  
- CEFR mapping  
- recommended lessons  

### 4.2 Business English Diagnostic
- workplace scenario

- functional language check  
- CEFR mapping

### 4.3 Corporate Diagnostic (Trainer Edition)
- role‑specific questions

- coaching‑alignment output  

---

## 5. Micro‑Task Library

### 5.1 Grammar Micro‑Tasks
- verb tense selection  
- modal usage  
- conditionals  
- articles  

### 5.2 Vocabulary Micro‑Tasks
- synonyms  
- collocations  
- workplace terminology  
- functional phrases  

### 5.3 Speaking Micro‑Tasks
- describe a problem  
- summarise a situation  
- propose a solution  

---

## 6. Error Pattern Analysis

Diagnostic flows must detect:
- tense errors  
- article errors  
- preposition errors  
- word‑choice errors  
- register errors  
- fluency vs accuracy imbalance  

---

## 7. CEFR Mapping Rules

### 7.1 A‑Level Indicators
- short answers  
- limited vocabulary  
- high error density  

### 7.2 B‑Level Indicators
- connected speech  
- moderate accuracy  
- functional communication  

### 7.3 C‑Level Indicators
- extended discourse  
- high accuracy  
- nuance and register control  

---

## 8. Recommendation Engine

Outputs:
- recommended CEFR level  
- recommended domain  
- recommended edition  
- recommended lesson sequence  
- recommended flow pack  
- recommended templates  

---

## 9. Validation Rules

Diagnostic flows must:
- follow lesson rules  
- follow edition rules  
- follow domain isolation  
- avoid bias  
- avoid cultural assumptions  

Forbidden:
- real company data  
- personal data retention  
- legal/financial advice  

---

## 10. Summary
The Diagnostic Flow Pack provides structured, CEFR‑aligned diagnostic processes that generate:

- level estimates  
- skill‑gap profiles  
- domain recommendations  
- lesson‑sequence recommendations  

It is fully compliant with system invariants and Phase‑4 extension rules.
