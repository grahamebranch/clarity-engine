# Phase‑4 Evolution Roadmap — Clarity Engine

## 1. Purpose
Phase‑4 defines how the Clarity Engine expands beyond its initial operational state.  
This roadmap identifies the **first set of extensions** that can be safely added without violating:

- system invariants  
- governance  
- meta rules  
- domain isolation  
- edition isolation  

Phase‑4 is the **evolution and extension layer**.

---

## 2. Phase‑4 Expansion Categories

### 2.1 New Domain Packs
Domains that can be added safely:
- Business English  
- Academic English  
- Travel English  
- Customer Service  
- Hospitality  
- Tech & Engineering  
- Medical English (restricted)  

Each domain requires:
- domain rules  
- domain templates  
- domain flows  
- domain vocabulary sets  

---

### 2.2 New Editions
Potential new editions:
- Corporate Trainer Edition  
- Executive Edition  
- Youth Edition  
- Accessibility Edition  
- Assessment‑Only Edition  

Each edition requires:
- tone rules  
- expansion rules  
- suppression rules  
- formatting rules  

---

### 2.3 New Flow Packs
Flows that can be added:
- onboarding flows  
- diagnostic flows  
- placement test flows  
- multi‑lesson programmes  
- business workflows (e.g., export pipelines)  

---

### 2.4 New Template Packs
Templates that can be added:
- business templates  
- academic templates  
- cultural deep‑dive templates  
- assessment templates  
- revision templates  

---

### 2.5 New Structural Packs
Structures that can be added:
- micro‑lessons  
- intensive lessons  
- asynchronous lessons  
- blended learning structures  

---

## 3. Phase‑4 Priorities

### Priority 1 — Business English Domain Pack
Reason:
- highest commercial value  
- highest adoption potential  
- aligns with operational parameters  
- minimal risk  

### Priority 2 — Corporate Trainer Edition
Reason:
- complements Business English  
- high enterprise value  
- minimal structural risk  

### Priority 3 — Diagnostic Flow Pack
Reason:
- improves onboarding  
- improves retention  
- improves lesson personalisation  

### Priority 4 — Assessment Template Pack
Reason:
- supports CEFR alignment  
- supports multi‑lesson programmes  

---

## 4. Phase‑4 Constraints

### 4.1 System‑Layer Constraints
No Phase‑4 extension may:
- modify architecture  
- modify philosophy  
- modify governance  
- modify meta  
- modify runtime  
- modify interfaces  

### 4.2 Operational Constraints
Extensions must:
- follow lesson rules  
- follow edition rules  
- follow domain isolation  
- follow edition isolation  

### 4.3 Validation Constraints
All extensions must pass:
- OSF  
- DIS  
- governance  
- edition validation  
- domain validation
