# Corporate Trainer Edition (v1) — Clarity Engine

## 1. Purpose
The Corporate Trainer Edition extends the Clarity Engine with a new edition type designed for:

- corporate training departments  
- L&D teams  
- HR training units  
- enterprise coaching environments  
- professional development programmes  

This edition adapts lesson content for **trainers working inside organisations**, not for learners.

It is a Phase‑4 extension and does not modify:
- system invariants  
- lesson structure  
- CEFR rules  
- governance  
- edition isolation  

---

## 2. Edition Identity

### 2.1 Name
**Corporate Trainer Edition**

### 2.2 Audience
- internal corporate trainers  
- L&D specialists  
- HR training managers  
- enterprise coaches  
- team leads delivering internal workshops  

### 2.3 Purpose
To provide:
- deeper instructional guidance  
- performance‑oriented framing  
- behavioural outcomes  
- coaching strategies  
- organisational alignment  

---

## 3. Edition Invariants

Corporate Trainer Edition must:
- preserve lesson structure  
- preserve semantic meaning  
- preserve CEFR alignment  
- preserve vocabulary and idioms  
- remain edition‑isolated  
- remain domain‑agnostic  

It may **expand**, but never **override**, the semantic core.

---

## 4. Tone & Voice

### 4.1 Tone
- professional  
- strategic  
- instructional  
- performance‑focused  

### 4.2 Voice
- confident  
- concise  
- outcome‑driven  
- aligned with organisational goals  

---

## 5. Content Expansions

Corporate Trainer Edition must include:

### 5.1 Performance Outcomes
Each lesson must specify:
- behavioural outcomes  
- communication outcomes  
- performance indicators  
- workplace application points  

### 5.2 Coaching Strategies
Each section must include:
- coaching prompts  
- observation cues  
- feedback strategies  
- reinforcement techniques  

### 5.3 Organisational Alignment
Each lesson must include:
- relevance to workplace tasks  
- alignment with team goals  
- cross‑functional applicability  
- suggested follow‑up tasks  

### 5.4 Risk & Sensitivity Notes
Trainers must be alerted to:
- cultural sensitivities  
- tone risks  
- miscommunication risks  
- escalation risks  

---

## 6. Content Suppressions

Corporate Trainer Edition must NOT include:
- learner‑facing simplifications  
- emotional reassurance language  
- informal tone  
- excessive scaffolding  
- personal anecdotes  

This edition is **trainer‑facing**, not learner‑facing.

---

## 7. Formatting Rules

### 7.1 Trainer Blocks
Trainer‑only content must be clearly marked:

**TRAINER NOTE:**  
**PERFORMANCE FOCUS:**  
**COACHING STRATEGY:**  
**RISK ALERT:**  

### 7.2 Structure
- bullet lists for strategies  
- numbered lists for sequences  
- bold for key actions  
- italics for examples  

### 7.3 Placement
Trainer notes must appear:
- at the end of each section  
- never inside learner‑facing text  

---

## 8. Edition Interaction

### 8.1 With Trainer Edition
Corporate Trainer Edition is **not** a replacement for Trainer Edition.  
It is a **specialised variant** with:

- more performance focus  
- more organisational alignment  
- more coaching detail  

### 8.2 With Client Edition
Corporate Trainer Edition must never include:
- learner‑facing tone  
- learner‑facing simplifications  

### 8.3 With Cultural Edition
Corporate Trainer Edition may include cultural notes **only when relevant to workplace communication**.

### 8.4 With Composite Edition
Composite Edition may borrow elements from Corporate Trainer Edition, but must not include full trainer detail.

---

## 9. Validation Rules

Corporate Trainer Edition must pass:

- OSF validation  
- DIS validation  
- governance validation  
- edition isolation validation  
- CEFR alignment validation  

Forbidden:
- altering lesson structure  
- altering semantic meaning  
- adding confidential corporate content  
- adding legal/financial advice  

---

## 10. Summary
The Corporate Trainer Edition provides a **performance‑focused, organisationally aligned, coaching‑oriented** version of each lesson.  
It is fully compliant with:

- system invariants  
- governance  
- meta rules  
- lesson rules  
- edition rules  

This is the second major extension in Phase‑4.
